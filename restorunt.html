"Restoran" is the word for restaurant in several languages, including Croatian, Serbian, Bosnian, and some Scandinavian languages.
It essentially means a place where people pay to sit and eat meals prepared and served on the premises.
The term "restaurant" itself originates from the French word "restaurer," meaning "to restore".
